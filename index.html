10 after 10 is a collections of projects that I am playing with.

The work for these projects occurs, for the most part, after my kids are asleep. As a result the majority of effort occurs after 10pm.

But really that's a bit of post hoc justification for the url. I registered the domain after thinking about the time '10:10', which is entertaining to me for a number of reasons, high among them the connection to the binary number 1010, which in decimal is 10.

The first project is this site itself. For now it is a github page which lets me experiment with jekyll and effectively grants me free hosting. It's going to grow and change over time.

Future projects are to be announced, but there will be 9 more.
